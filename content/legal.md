### 1. Privacy Policy
We believe in the "Small Web." This website is a static site designed to be read, not to track its readers.

* **No Cookies:** This site does not use cookies, trackers, or third-party analytics (like Google Analytics).
* **No Accounts:** We do not offer user accounts, logins, or newsletters. We do not collect your email address.
* **Server Logs:** This site is hosted on a static provider (e.g., GitHub Pages / Cloudflare). The hosting provider may log your IP address for security and abuse prevention purposes. We do not access or sell this data.

### 3. Copyright & Fair Dealing
This archive aggregates factual data regarding public interest events.

* **Factual Data:** The specific facts of a breach (Dates, Counts, Data Types) are not copyrightable.
* **Source Material:** Where we quote summaries or press releases, we do so under the principles of **Fair Dealing** for the purpose of reporting news and review (Copyright Act 1968).
