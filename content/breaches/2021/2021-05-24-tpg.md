## Summary

TPG Telecom confirmed in May 2021 that customer data discovered on the dark web belonged to its subscribers. The telecommunications provider acknowledged the breach after customer information was found being traded on underground forums.

## What Happened

Customer data associated with TPG Telecom services was discovered circulating on dark web marketplaces in May 2021. When contacted about the data, TPG confirmed it belonged to the company's customers, indicating an undisclosed breach had occurred.

The exact method of compromise and timeline of the breach were not publicly disclosed by TPG. The data surfaced on dark web forums where stolen databases are commonly traded among cybercriminals.

## Impact on Individuals

TPG customers whose data was exposed faced potential risks from having their information available on the dark web, where it could be used for identity theft, fraud, or targeted attacks. The specific number of affected customers was not publicly disclosed.

## Organisational Response

TPG Telecom confirmed the data belonged to its customers but provided limited public information about the scope of the breach, how it occurred, or remediation efforts undertaken.
