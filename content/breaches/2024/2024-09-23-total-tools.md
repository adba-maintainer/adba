## Summary

In September 2024, Australian hardware retailer Total Tools suffered a data breach exposing personal details of approximately 38,000 customers including names, email addresses, phone numbers, and physical addresses through a cyber attack.

## What Happened

Total Tools' systems were breached, allowing unauthorized access to customer contact information stored in their database.

## Impact on Individuals

38,000 Total Tools customers had contact details exposed, creating phishing and spam risks.

## Organisational Response

Total Tools acknowledged the breach and informed affected customers.
